# Warehouse Incident Backlog

Owner: Warehouse ops
SLA: 4 hours

1. Filter `/admin` incidents by `domain=warehouse` and identify critical vs aging backlog.
2. Validate impacted appointments, receipts, dispatches, and linked offers.
3. Check whether degraded warehouse mode is enabled.
4. Reassign incident owners, confirm evidence completeness, and unblock payment-related holds.
5. Escalate to holding ops if the backlog threatens SLA or settlement.
6. Close incidents only after timeline, evidence, and trip state match.
