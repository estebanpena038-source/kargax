# Support Escalation Source of Truth

This file is the canonical launch roster for support and escalation.

## Coverage model

- Support window: extended hours + critical on-call
- Primary on-call: Support Lead
- Backup on-call: Head of Ops
- Technical escalation: CTO / Founding Engineer
- Finance escalation: Finance Lead
- Executive escalation: CEO / Founder

## Contact channels

- Primary ticket intake: `/soporte`
- Admin working queue: `/admin`
- Critical incident bridge: internal on-call channel

## SLA defaults

- Critical: 5 minutes
- High: 30 minutes
- Medium: 4 hours
- Low: 1 business day

## Pre-launch checklist

- confirm the actual human assigned to each role above
- confirm the after-hours phone or chat channel
- confirm who approves rollback outside business hours
- confirm who owns first response for payment and wallet incidents
